Applications for the 2020 Innovation Camp are now open!

2020 Virtual Innovation Bootcamp

Innovate Ghana is an annual design competition and workshop that began in 2013 to challenge high school, tertiary students and unemployed graduates to develop solutions to national development issues. We do so by teaching principles of critical thinking, human-centered design, STEM/STEAM and entrepreneurship. The theme of this year's Virtual Innovation Camp, "Unlocking Innovation in a Global Crisis: Lessons from COVID-19" will address six core issues faced across the world today in a month-long, two-phased program.
